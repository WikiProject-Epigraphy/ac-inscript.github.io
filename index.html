Welcome!

This is the main page of my Doctoral Research at University of London concerning Epigraphy and Wikidata. This space is conceived as a living portfolio, a place where my research takes shape and leaves traces of its development. Here you can follow my journey through Digital Humanities and Epigraphy, Wikidata and Computing and other paths that branch out from my research, paths along which I am most often a learner, and occasionally a teacher. Along the way, you will also encounter a few “eXtras”, for one can easily understand how many collateral avenues open up when conducting research. ☾ When in doubt, go to the library.
